GenRest

The application to generate your web service with semantics annotations in Domotics, Transport, or Environment domains. Just give your specifications and the application give you your service in your favorite language. Finally, you still have to launch your application with our scripts already done.

Domotic
We have designed a data structure to represent building topology with semantic annotations. You can define your building or house with all their sensors and actuators. The goal is to get the possibility to access your sensor's data and update the state of your actuators by the way of internet.
Environment
With this feature, we have sought to give you the ability to access your sensor data around the world. You only have to define your specifications in order to get your application. But, that feature is not ready yet. But we do our best to make it ready. Keep patience please.
Transport
With this feature, we have sought to give you the ability to access data from your carriers and or containers, package. You will also be able to explore these data and analyze them more easily thanks to semantic annotations. But, that feature is not ready yet. But we do our best to make it ready. Keep patience please.
