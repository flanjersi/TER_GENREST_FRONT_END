Getting Started

Tutorial to create a domotic project:
  • How to create a new user account
  • How to create a new project
  • How to handle the tree-view
  • How to handle the tab-view
How to create a new user account
The first time you connect to the application, you have to create an user account, to do this please go to the top, and on the right side you have to click on:"SignIn" This action will make appear a pop-up named:"Authentification". As you don't have an user account you have to click on :"SignUp", this action will make appear a pop-up named: "SignUp" also, to make an account you have to fill out the form. Then you can click on: "SignIn"to connect.
How to create a new project

To create a new project yo have to connect first, then a new page will appear, and on the right you can add a new project by clicking on the button add , the button edit help you to edit and to modify you project's informations, the button delete will delete your project; you can have many projects as you want.

To create your domotic specifications you have to click on the project you create first, then a new page will appear, this page contains on the right side a tree-view and on the left side a tab-view.

How to handle the tree-view
The tree-view is the place where you will do all the specifications of your domotic project, it appears after you click on your project.
You have to follow some steps to full fill your domotic project:

  • Create a new building:
    • The first step is to create a building, to do this you have to click on the button add next the field called "Building", after that you have to specify some informations asked in the pop-up, you can after modify the informations specified by clicking on the button edit

  • Create a new floor:
    • After the building, you have to specify a floor, a building can have one or many floor.
    To create, or modify the floor you have to follow the same instructions like as the creation of building but on clicking on the button named "Floor".

  • Create a new zone:
    • After the creation of the floor, you have to specify the zone; a floor contains one or many zone, or corridor.
    To create, or modify the zone you have to follow the same instructions like as the creation of building but on clicking on the button named "Zone".

  • Create a new room:
    • After the creation of the zone, you have to specify the room, a room contains none, one or many actuator and sensor.
    To create, or modify the room you have to follow the same instructions like as the creation of building but on clicking on the button named "Room".

  • Create a new corridor:
    • After the creation of the zone, you have to specify the corridor, a corridor contains none, one or many actuator and sensor.
    To create, or modify the corridor you have to follow the same instructions like as the creation of building but on clicking on the button named "Corridor".

  • Create a new actuator:
    • After the creation of the room or corridor, you have to specify the actuator.
    To create, or modify an actuator you have to follow the same instructions like as the creation of building but on clicking on the button named "Actuator".

  • Create a new sensor:
    • After the creation of the room or corridor, you have to specify the sensor.
    To create, or modify a sensor you have to follow the same instructions like as the creation of building but on clicking on the button named "Sensor".

Like you see the tree-view shows an tree structure of your project, starting with the building to actuators and sensors, the tab-view on the left-side will show you the hierachy of your project, and the specifications you made.

If you are satisfied with the specifications you have entered for your building, you can press the: "Deploy" button to create your API.

How to handle the tab-view
The tab-view is here to show the specifications of your building, it will offer a visualization of your project:

First, you have the tab named "Graphical visualization", when you click on it, it allows you to visualize an hierarchical view of your project specification, starting with the building to sensor and actuator, all the modifications you make in the tree-view are seen at real-time in this graph.

Second, you have the tab named "Project specification", when you click on it, it allows you to visualize a specification of the component of your building, to do that you have just to click in the tree-view on the component you want to visualize such as a building, floor, corridor, room...

Third, you have the tab named "Json visualization", when you click on it, it allows you to visualize the Json-ld view of the current entity specifications with the semantic annotations.
